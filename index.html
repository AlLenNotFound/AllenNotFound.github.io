About

I am a Master's student in High Performance Computing at Politecnico di Milano. My research interests include privacy and memorization in large language models, agent security, high-performance computing, and quantitative research.

I am interested in understanding how modern machine learning systems memorize, expose, and leak sensitive information, and in developing reliable evaluation methods for privacy and security risks in AI systems.
